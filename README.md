## 1. 🫀 ML Myocardial Infarction
[👉 Click here to view Full Code & Results](./myocardial-infarction/myocardial-infarction.md)

- Myocardial infarction (heart attack) is a leading cause of death globally, often leading to severe complications like heart failure and cardiogenic shock. Early prediction of outcomes is critical for timely clinical intervention. Machine learning (ML) methods such as Logistic Regression, SVM, Random Forest, and XGBoost have shown effectiveness in predicting MI-related mortality.
- This study uses the UCI Myocardial Infarction Complications dataset: 1,700 patients, 124 clinical features, with 15,974 missing values. The study aims to identify the best imputation method, handle class imbalance, build and compare ML models for predicting mortality, and identify the most influential clinical predictors at the time of ER admission.
